# Nike Retail Sales Analytics Project

## Overview

This project analyzes Nike retail sales performance across multiple U.S. regions using data visualization and business analytics techniques. The objective of the project was to identify trends in regional sales performance, evaluate differences between sales methods, and develop strategic recommendations to improve in-store sales in underperforming regions.

The analysis specifically focuses on Nike’s declining in-store sales performance in the Southern region and proposes marketing and promotional solutions supported by data visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Nike’s in-store sales in the Southern region appear to underperform compared to other regions due to:

* Increased focus on online sales
* Limited localized marketing
* Reduced in-store engagement
* Lack of innovative promotional strategies

The goal of this project was to use data visualization to identify performance trends and recommend actionable business solutions.

---

## Key Visualizations

### Regional Sales Performance

A stacked bar chart was created to compare total sales performance across regions and sales methods, including:

* In-store sales
* Online sales
* Outlet sales

The visualization revealed weaker in-store sales performance in the Southern region compared to regions such as the West and Northeast.

### In-Store Sales by Product in the South

A product-level breakdown was used to analyze which product categories generated the strongest in-store sales within the Southern region.

The visualization showed that footwear categories significantly outperformed apparel categories in Southern in-store sales.

### State-Level Men’s Apparel Sales

A geographic visualization highlighted differences in men’s apparel sales across Southern states.

Texas demonstrated the strongest sales performance among the states analyzed, indicating potential opportunities for regional marketing expansion.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the findings, the project proposed several strategies to improve Nike’s Southern retail performance:

* Collaborating with regional athletes and influencers
* Hosting in-person promotional events
* Creating limited-time retail sales campaigns
* Increasing localized community engagement
* Strengthening regional brand visibility

These recommendations were designed to increase in-store traffic and improve customer engagement.
